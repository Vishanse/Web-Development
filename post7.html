Check https status of your website using httpstatus.io

This httpstatus.io website helps you to detect the status of your website by displaying your status codes

Watch the Tutorial

STEPS

1: Go to httpstatus.io

2: Click Home tab.

3: Enter your url (eg: www.sitewebdevelopment.com)

4: Put a tick mark for Canonical domain check.

5: Press Check Status button.

Now httpsstatus.io will show the status codes of your website.
