How to Check HTTP Status Codes Using httpstatus.io

Step 1: Open your browser and go to httpstatus.io.

Step 2: Enter the URL of the website you want to check in the input box.

Step 3: Choose whether to check a single URL or multiple URLs at once by separating them with commas or line breaks.

Step 4: Click the Check URL(s) button.

Step 5: Review the results. You will see the HTTP status codes (like 200, 301, 404, 500) for each URL and the response headers.
