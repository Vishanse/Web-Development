How to Use BrowserStack to Check Device Compatibility

Ensure your GitHub Pages website works perfectly on all devices and browsers by testing it with BrowserStack. This tutorial helps you detect layout issues, browser-specific bugs, and enhance your website’s user experience.

Step 1: Go to BrowserStack and create a free or paid account.

Step 2: Log in and select the type of testing you want: Live (real-time interactive testing) or Automate (automated testing).

Step 3: Choose the operating system, device, and browser combination you want to test your website on.

Step 4: Enter your website URL and start testing. BrowserStack will load your site on a real device/browser combination so you can check layout, responsiveness, and functionality.

Step 5: Interact with your website as if you were using the actual device. Test navigation, forms, buttons, media, and any animations.

Step 6: Take screenshots or record sessions for reference or bug reporting.

Screenshot placeholder: BrowserStack interface showing your website on multiple devices

Tip: Always test on the most popular devices and browsers for your target audience to ensure your site looks and works perfectly everywhere.
