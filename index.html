Step 2: Upload Your Website to GitHub

Open your project in VS Code or your preferred editor. Create a new repository on GitHub, then upload your website files. Once uploaded, enable GitHub Pages in repository settings to make your website live.

Watch the Tutorial

Step 3: Connect Your Domain to GitHub

Once your website is live on GitHub Pages, you can connect your custom domain (like yourwebsite.com) so visitors can access it easily. Go to your repository’s Settings → Pages → Custom Domain, enter your domain name, and update your domain’s DNS records as instructed by GitHub. Your website will now be reachable via your own domain.
